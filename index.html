What is BandAid?

BandAid is a site aimed at providing information around mental health and different forms of support. The site also provides numbers for emergency contacts in times of crisis. We are devoted to ending the stigma around mental health for everyone.

Rembember: You are never alone.

Tip's to manage your mental health:

  • Exercise regularly
  • Get enough sleep
  • Eat a healthy diet
  • Practice mindfulness
  • Stay connected with others
  • Seek professional help if needed
