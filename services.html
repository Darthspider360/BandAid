Therapies

There is no shame in seeking help for your mental health.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) aims to you explore and change how you think about your life. It helps to free yourself from unhelpful patterns of behaviour by creating goals and task's to carry out.

Learn More
Psychodynamic psychotherapy

Psychodynamic psychotherapy looks at how childhood experiences and thoughts you're not aware of (your unconscious mind) affect your thinking, feelings, relationships and behaviour today.

Learn More
Animal-assisted therapy

Animal-assisted therapy involves interacting with animals as well as a therapist to help you explore your feelings and experiences in a different way. Including animals can help you open up and feel more at ease

Learn More
Creative therapies

Creative therapies enable you to express yourself in a variety of ways, often without using words, such as through painting, drawing, photography, dance, music or drama. This can support you to understand yourself better.
